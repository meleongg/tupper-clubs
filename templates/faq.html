FAQs

Clubs are a new concept for many new students.

Here are some frequently asked questions.

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact us!

How do I start a club?

To start a club, all you need is a sponsor teacher and a club idea! After you get your sponsor teacher, let student council know that your club is running!

Am I allowed to start a club anytime during the year?

Yes! Just follow the instructions in starting a club!

Can clubs get support from student council for future events?

Yes! Just make sure to let the president and the vice president know.

How do I register/join a club?

You can sign up using this website, or you can sign up for clubs in person during club's day!

Can I join a club anytime during the year?

Yes! Just contact the club president or the club sponsor teacher to join!
